Biography

I hail from a small town in Southern Wisconsin. I graduated with a BA from Carroll University in 2015 and started my professional career in Milwaukee Wisconsin with a public accounting firm.

On a personal note, when I’m not deep into a project I can be found teaching a yoga class at a studio around the city, hiking the trails with my spirited border collie, chatting it up over brunch, or jamming with fellow musicians. People say I’m vibrant and dedicated.

While my professional background is in finance, I’ve always had a deep connection to arts. I grew up performing on stage in dance, choral groups, piano and vocal opportunities, etc.
