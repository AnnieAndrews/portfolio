Associated Bank

Mobile | Redesign | Prototype

View prototype

Associated Bank is a midwestern regional bank, serving over 100 communities in Wisconsin, Illinois, and Minnesota. The Bank has different channels for customers to conduct their business to their convenience including in-branch, mobile and online banking. Across these platforms, Associated Bank has maintained open communication with customers to share feedback regarding their experiences which are used to help shape technology improvements and enhance products. Having been both an employee and customer of the bank, I was inspired to recreate the mobile interface. In its current form the application is functional and very useful, however, I wanted to see if I could take it to the next level to more clearly guide users through their desired actions brought together through a more detailed visual design.

I was not able to get in touch with any members of the Bank’s team working on UX/UI design on any platform. Had I been able to, I would have asked about current limitations and challenges facing the design team so to better understand the scope and constraints of such a project.

For the scope of my project, I focused on rebranding and redesigning the features already included in the mobile banking application.

Here is a look into the mobile app as it is currently:

I put together a brand that represented the Bank's confident and trustworthy nature. The logo and wordmark were brought to a more modern, appealing look.

In putting together the final design, I was sure to use the branding while creating a more fluid experience. The design is now inline with iOS guidelines, using the Apple features that already exist to create an easy understanable interface.
